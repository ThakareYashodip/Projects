Essential Health Information

Understanding your health metrics is just the beginning. Here's what you need to know about maintaining optimal wellness.

BMI Ranges

Underweight: <18.5
Normal: 18.5-24.9
Overweight: 25-29.9
Obese: ≥30

BMR Facts

Your BMR accounts for 60-75% of daily calorie burn. It decreases with age and increases with muscle mass.

Calorie Goals

A 500-calorie daily deficit leads to ~1lb/week weight loss. Always consult professionals for personalized advice.

Track Progress

Regular monitoring helps maintain healthy habits. Combine metrics with lifestyle changes for best results.
